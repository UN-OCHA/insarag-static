USAR Coordination (UC) Manual

 

This section contains the UC manual which provides guidance to train and prepare USAR teams in operating the Reception and Departure Centre (RDC), the Sector Coordination Cell (SCC) and the USAR Coordination Cell – a cell within the On Site Operations Coordination Centre (OSOCC) that works with the OSOCC and affected nation to coordinates the national and international USAR response.
