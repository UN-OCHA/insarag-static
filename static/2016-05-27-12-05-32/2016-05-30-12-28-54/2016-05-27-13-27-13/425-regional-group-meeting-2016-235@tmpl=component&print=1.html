REGIONAL GROUP MEETING 2009 - HUNGARY

The 2009 annual meeting of the INSARAG Regional Group Africa/Europe/Middle East was held in Budapest, Hungary on 01-02 October 2009. The meeting was hosted by the Government of Hungary (the National Directorate General for Disaster Management of Ministry of Interior).
