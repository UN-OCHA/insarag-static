REGIONAL GROUP MEETING 2000 - SWITZERLAND

The fifth meeting of the International Search and Rescue Advisory Group (INSARAG) Regional Group Africa/Europe was organized by the Tunisian Civil Protection in Hammamet, Tunisia, from 16 to 19 November 2000 and funded by the Swiss Government. The Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (OCHA), in its capacity as INSARAG Secretariat, co-organized the meeting.
