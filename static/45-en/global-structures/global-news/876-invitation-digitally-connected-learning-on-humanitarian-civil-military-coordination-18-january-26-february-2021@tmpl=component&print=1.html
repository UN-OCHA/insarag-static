Invitation: Digitally Connected Learning on Humanitarian Civil-Military Coordination, 18 January - 26 February 2021

 

Invitation to the First Digitally Connected Learning on Humanitarian Civil-Military Coordination.

 

The virtual event will be held from 18 January to 26 February 2021. Please help us share the information widely with your networks and among interested colleagues.

 

Applications are welcome before Monday 14 December 2020 at https://vosocc.unocha.org/Reg/srvy453_UN-CMCoord_DCL1.html 

 

Please find attached our invitation letter together with the weekly programme. Additional information is also available on our website at https://sites.google.com/dialoguing.org/home/training-and-partnership/digitally-connected-learning 

 

The Digitally Connected Learning on Humanitarian Civil-Military Coordination comprises 5-6 hours of mandatory individual/group work per week over a period of six weeks. Upon full and active participation, graduates will receive a certificate equal to the residential UN-CMCoord course and be considered with priority for a residential UN-CMCoord Skills Training.

  

For any questions or support with the application process, please contact Anne-Sophie Allegre (allegre@un.org )
