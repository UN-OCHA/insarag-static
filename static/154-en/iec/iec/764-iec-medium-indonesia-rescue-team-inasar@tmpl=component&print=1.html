IEC Medium Indonesia Rescue Team (INASAR)

The Indonesian Rescue Medium Team has successfully undergone an intensive field exercise as per the External Classification of USAR Team under the INSARAG guidelines from 25 to 29 November 2019 in Indonesia.

A professional group of experts from New Zealand, Malaysia, Japan, US, Australia, Russia Federation and UK (and with Singapore mentor) participated as classifiers in INSARAG's flagship and unique peer review and quality assurance process.

Congratulations to the Indonesian Rescue Team!
