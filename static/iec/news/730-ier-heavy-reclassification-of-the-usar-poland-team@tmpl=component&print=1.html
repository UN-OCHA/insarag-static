IER Heavy reclassification of the USAR Poland Team

The USAR Heavy Team of Poland  has successfully undergone an intensive field exercise as per the External Classification of USAR Team under the INSARAG guidelines from 10 to 14 June 2019 in Pstrąże, Poland

A professional group of experts from Japan, United States, United Arab Emirates, Germany, Lithuania, Hungary, France, Germany and Switzerland (and with United Kingdom mentor) participated as classifiers in INSARAG's flagship and unique peer review and quality assurance process.

Congratulation to USAR.Poland
