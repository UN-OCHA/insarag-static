IECR Training Course for Team Leaders, Mentors and INSARAG Secretariat Representatives

INSARAG IEC/R Team Leaders, Mentors and Secretariat Representative Training was conducted at the Sultanate of Oman Public Authority for Civil Defense and Ambulance (PACDA) in Muscat, Oman from 10 -13 February 2020. The Training Working Group (TWG) together with representatives from Team Leaders, Information Management Working Group (IMWG), Light Team Working Group (LTWG) and Medical Working Group (MWG), prepared the course throughout 2019. The revised training now includes the recent changes. The 18 participants from 12 countries and OCHA shared their experience in IEC/Rs, and mutually learned to take leadership roles in the classification system.
