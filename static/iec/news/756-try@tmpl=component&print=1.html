IEC Heavy classification of China International Search and Rescue Team (CHN -2 CSAR)

China's International Search and Rescue (CHN -2 CSAR) Team has successfully undergone an intensive 36 hours full-scale and demanding peer-review field exercise as per the INSARAG External Classification of USAR Team under the INSARAG guidelines, from 20  to 24 October 2019 in China.

A  group of experts from Germany, Malaysia, Australia, Korea, Japan, Hungary, Italy, France, USA and Russia Federation and led by a Singaporean mentor participated as classifiers in the INSARAG's flagship and unique peer review and quality assurance process.

Congratulation to CHN -2 CSAR
