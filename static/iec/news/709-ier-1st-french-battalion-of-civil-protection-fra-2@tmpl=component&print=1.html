IER Heavy 1st French Battalion of Civil Protection (FRA 2)

The INSARAG Secretariat congratulates the French team HUSAR FRA2 _ UIICS 1 (1st Batallion) for its successful reclassification as Heavy team. The INSARAG External Reclassification took place from 25-29 March 2019 between Brignoles and Sancoins (France) and ended on Friday 28 March with an official ceremony. We thank their mentor, Peter Goxharaj, from THW Germany for his support, as well as all the seven classifiers, coming from Armenia, Jordan, Sweden, Belgium and Switzerland, led by Marcus Bock from Austria
