IER Heavy 7e French Battalion of Civil Protection (FRA3)

Last week the French 03 HUSAR team confirmed its membership in the INSARAG network, after having successfully completed the INSARAG External Reclassification. The reclassification took place from 1 to 5 April 2019 in Sancoins (France). After a 36-hour long exercise, the classifiers team led by Martin Evers from The Netherlands confirmed that FRA 03 meets the requirements as Heavy USAR team.

Félicitations à l’équipe!
