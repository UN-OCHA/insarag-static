IEC Medium Pakistan Rescue Team

The Pakistan Rescue Team has successfully undergone an intensive field exercise as per the External Classification of USAR Team under the INSARAG guidelines from 28 to 31 October 2019 in Pakistan.

A professional group of experts from Germany, Jordan, Austria, Morocco, and United Kingdom (and with United Kingdom mentor) participated as classifiers in INSARAG's flagship and unique peer review and quality assurance process.

Congratulations to Pakistan Rescue Team!
