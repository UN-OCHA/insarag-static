IER Heavy The Netherlands (USAR.NL)

The second IER USAR.NL of The Netherlands Heavy Team has successfully undergone an intensive field exercise as per the External Classification of USAR Team under the INSARAG guidelines from 25 to 27 January 2019 in Tinglev, Denmark, in a joint event with the MODEX Exercise.

A professional group of experts from Austria, Singapore, France, Estonia, Switzerland, United Kingdom and Turkey (and with Danish mentor) participated as classifiers in INSARAG's flagship and unique peer review and quality assurance process.

Congratulation to USAR.NL
