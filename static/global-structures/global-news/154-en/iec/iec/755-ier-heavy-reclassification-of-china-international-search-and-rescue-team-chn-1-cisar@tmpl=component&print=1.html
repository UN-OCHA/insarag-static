IER Heavy reclassification of China International Search and Rescue Team (CHN -1 CISAR)

China's International Search and Rescue Team (CHN -1 CISAR) Team has successfully undergone an intensive 36 hours full-scale and demanding peer-review field exercise as per the  INSARAG External Reclassification of USAR Team under the INSARAG guidelines, from 20  to 24 October 2019 in China.

A  group of experts from New Zealand, Singapore, Australia, Germany, South Korea, United Kingdom, Japan, and Jordan and led by an Australian mentor participated as classifiers in the INSARAG's flagship and unique peer review and quality assurance process.

Congratulation to CHN -1 CISAR
