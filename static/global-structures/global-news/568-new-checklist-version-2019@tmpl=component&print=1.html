New checklist version 2019

Following the INSARAG Steering Group(ISG) Meeting 2019 in February, INSARAG teams undergoing the IEC/R from February 2019 will have to demonstrate USAR Coordination and use of technologies for data collection/analysis using Information Management tools and systems.

The self-explanatory updated and improved checklist attached, and designed by the INSARAG Secretariat facilitates the work of the teams and classifiers. Please note that the ISG directed that all IEC/Rs from 1st February 2019 this checklist is IN FORCE and team have to adopt this checklist in their respective classification/re-classification exercises effective from 7 Feb. 2019.

If you have further questions on this, please contact the Secretariat at insarag@un.org

Thank you.
