OSOCC e-learning course

We are pleased to announce the launch of the OSOCC E-Learning Training Course by the Field Coordination Support Section (FCSS) in OCHA Geneva. We very much encourage you to complete this course, and also to forward the links below to Urban Search and Rescue (USAR) teams and National Management Disaster Authorities (NDMAs) in your country or region.

The OSOCC Awareness E-Learning course includes three modules.

Start the course by clicking on Module 1: https://vosocc.unocha.org//ELearning/OSOCCAwarenessModule1/story.html
When completed, please close down the module.


Click here for Module 2: https://vosocc.unocha.org//ELearning/OSOCCAwarenessModule2/story.html

When completed, please close down the module.


Click here for Module 3: https://vosocc.unocha.org//ELearning/OSOCCAwarenessModule3/story.html

At the end of module 3, you will be directed to a link on the Virtual OSOCC, where you can print your certificate.

Note: Browsers for Viewing the published OSOCC e-learning Awareness Course include Adobe Flash Player 10.3 https://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/ or these web browsers: Windows: Firefox (latest version) recommended, Google Chrome (latest version), Internet Explorer 8 and later, Microsoft Edge (latest version), Mac: Safari 7 and later, Google Chrome (latest version)
