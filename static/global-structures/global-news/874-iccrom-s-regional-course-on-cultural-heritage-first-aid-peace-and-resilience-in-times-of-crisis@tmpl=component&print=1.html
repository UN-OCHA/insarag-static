ICCROM's Regional Course on Cultural Heritage First Aid, Peace and Resilience in Times of Crisis

The ICCROM's upcoming Regional Course on Cultural Heritage First Aid, Peace and Resilience in Times of Crisis is targeted at professionals who are engaged and motivated to bring about change to preserve their heritage - it is open to emergency responders, humanitarian and cultural heritage professionals, military personnel and disaster risk management specialists from the MENAP region.

 

If you wish to join, please apply.
